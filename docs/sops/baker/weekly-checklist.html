Weekly Checklist

Before Opening Orders

  • ☐Update this week's menu.
  • ☐Check names, prices, descriptions, and photos.
  • ☐Choose what is live or featured.
  • ☐Check the public menu.
  • ☐Open ordering only when everything looks right.

While Orders Are Open

  • ☐Check paid orders.
  • ☐Look for customer notes.
  • ☐Watch for items selling fast.
  • ☐Close ordering if the week feels too full.

Before Ordering Closes

  • ☐Review paid orders.
  • ☐Check pickup and delivery counts.
  • ☐Check for missing delivery details.

Prep Day

  • ☐Review paid orders.
  • ☐Check item totals.
  • ☐Check pickup orders.
  • ☐Check delivery orders and addresses.

Bake And Fulfillment Day

  • ☐Use the pickup queue.
  • ☐Use the delivery page.
  • ☐Send customer updates if used.
  • ☐Mark orders complete after handoff.

After Fulfillment

  • ☐Check active lists are clear.
  • ☐Review issue orders.
  • ☐Note fixes for next week.

If you are unsure, close ordering first. Then check the public menu and ask Chandler for help.
