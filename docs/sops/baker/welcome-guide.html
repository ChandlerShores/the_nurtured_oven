Welcome Guide

The bakery website helps you show the weekly menu, accept orders, review paid orders, and manage pickup and delivery day.

The admin area is your quiet workspace. Customers do not see it.

What Customers See

Customers see the public website. They can view the menu, place orders when ordering is open, and get updates about their order.

What You Do In The Admin Area

  • Update this week's menu.
  • Open or close ordering.
  • Review paid orders.
  • Check pickup and delivery lists.
  • Send short customer updates when needed.

Learn These First

  1. Update the weekly menu.
  2. Open or close ordering.
  3. Review paid orders.
  4. Use the pickup and delivery pages on bake day.

What To Ignore At First

You do not need to learn every area right away. Start with the weekly menu, ordering control, and orders list.

The Safety Rule

If you are unsure, turn ordering off first. That gives you time to check the menu, review orders, and ask for help before more customers order.

Success Looks Like

  • You know where to make menu changes.
  • You know where to check paid orders.
  • You know how to stop new orders if something feels wrong.
  • You know to check the public menu when you are done.
