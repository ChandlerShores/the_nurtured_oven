Emergency Guide

Close ordering first if new customers should not order yet. This gives you time to check things calmly.

Then Check

  • The public menu.
  • The paid orders list.
  • Pickup and delivery counts.
  • Any order marked as an issue.

Do Not

  • Do not delete paid orders casually.
  • Do not mark an order complete before it is truly complete.
  • Do not send a customer update if you are unsure what to say.

Common Situations

Sold Out Faster Than Expected

Close ordering if the whole week is full. If only one item is sold out, mark that item sold out. Check the public menu when you are done.

Menu Looks Wrong

Close ordering first. Go to Menu and check the item. Save the change, then check the public menu.

Customer Says They Paid But The Order Is Missing

Do not create a replacement order casually. Ask the customer for their payment receipt or order note. Then ask Chandler to help check it.

Wrong Pickup Or Delivery Info

Open the order and read the details. Contact the customer if needed. Do not mark the order complete until the handoff is clear.

Too Many Orders Came In

Close ordering. Review paid orders. Decide what can still be fulfilled. Contact affected customers if something must change.

Cannot Fulfill An Item

Close ordering if more orders would make the problem worse. Find affected paid orders and contact those customers with a short, kind note.
