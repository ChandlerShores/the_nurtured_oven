Bake Day Checklist

Start Of Day

  • ☐Open Orders.
  • ☐Review paid orders.
  • ☐Look for customer notes and dietary notes.
  • ☐Check issue orders.

Pickup Orders

  • ☐Open Pickup.
  • ☐Review the queue.
  • ☐Send pickup notes when orders are truly ready.
  • ☐Mark Picked up only after handoff.

Delivery Orders

  • ☐Open Deliveries.
  • ☐Check addresses and phone numbers.
  • ☐Review the delivery plan.
  • ☐Mark delivered only after drop-off.

Customer Updates

  • ☐Keep messages short.
  • ☐Choose the right customer.
  • ☐Check the message log after sending.

If something feels wrong, pause. Do not change paid orders casually. Ask Chandler for help.
